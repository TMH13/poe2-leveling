Worth knowing before you route

  • How it's sorted: rows are ordered by the zone's area level within each act (acts are sequential level bands: 1–14, 14–29, 30–42, 42–52, 52–58). Ranges like "13–14" are area levels; sorting uses the lower value.
  • Books of Specialisation give 2 passive points and unlock 2 weapon-set points each — right-click to consume. Collect them all and you should reach 24 weapon-set points per set by the end of the Interludes.
  • Changeable later: only Ancient Vows, the Seven Pillars (Orbala), and Goddess of Justice can be re-picked. The Venom Crypts / Servi choice and everything else is locked once chosen.
  • All buffs are missable — they come from optional bosses/side quests.
  • Runeforging gate: the Act 1 Lost Catacombs vault must be cleared to unlock the Verisium Anvil — later acts won't open it on their own.
  • Act 4 skill points: this newest act varies by source — Game8 lists a "Dark Mists" Book at Kedge Bay, while the live 0.5 route credits Omniphobia (Journey's End). Either way Act 4 grants +4 skill points total (the other +2 is Trial of the Ancestors). Verify in-game.
